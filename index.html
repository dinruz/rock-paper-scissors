RULES

🟡 It's You vs. The Machine!

Welcome!

This is a simple, 3-choice game.
You will play against the PC until one of you wins the match.

🟡 The Logic

In each round, choose one of three hand shapes.
Each shape can beat only one other shape (1 pt.):

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock

Matching moves result in a Tie (0 pts.).

🟡 The Winner

The first player to reach 5 points wins the game.
